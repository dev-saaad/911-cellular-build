Your Safety App Questions, Answered

Does this come with a warranty?
Yes, the Wall Mounted Panic Buttons have a 1-year limited warranty that protects against manufacturer defects.
Can the BadgeR+ connect to enterprise WiFi networks?
Yes, the BadgeR+ hardware supports 802.1x enterprise WiFi networks.
Do they have cellular connection capabilities?
No, the device only connects via WiFi to your existing access points.
Do you need to come to our facilities to install anything?
No, the 911Cellular back-end web portal is cloud-based and thus no on-site servers or installation is required. The wall mounted units themselves are shipped with easy installation instructions.
Is there an API?
Yes, the 911Cellular API is accessible at no additional cost to customers.
How long does it take to get this up and running?
Most clients who purchase our Wall Mounted Panic Buttons are up and running within 45 days of contract execution. After contract execution, all customers are introduced to a dedicated Onboarding Specialist who will guide them through the process of implementation. Units will be shipped from our facilities promptly.
How much do these units cost?
Total cost varies depending on how many units you require. Once we get this information from you and learn more about your environment, our security experts will provide you with a personalized quote.
