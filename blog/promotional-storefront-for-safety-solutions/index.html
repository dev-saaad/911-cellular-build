New in 2025: Amplify Awareness About Your Safety Solutions with Our Curated Promotional Storefront!

Written by 911 cellular, Jan 3, 2025

At 911Cellular, we’re not just about cutting-edge safety and emergency communication solutions—we’re all about transforming your sense of security! We believe that true safety stems from awareness, preparedness and accessibility. That’s why we’re thrilled to unveil our exclusive promotional storefront, now available to our valued clients! Dive into a curated range of promotional products designed to empower your community and keep everyone safe and connected like never before! Get ready to elevate your safety game!

What’s in the Store?

We’ve carefully curated a selection of items aimed at boosting product visibility and understanding:

  • Signage, Posters, and Flyers: Keep critical safety information front and center.
  • Branded Table Covers: Use these event materials to increase exposure at conferences and expos.
  • Keyboard Panic Button Stickers: Clearly mark computers with panic button software for quick and easy access in an emergency.
  • Promo Items: Branded water bottles, mugs, chapstick, flashlights and hand sanitizer to distribute during events or meetings.
  • Office Supplies: Practical items like pens, post-it notes and notepads to reinforce awareness.
  • Apparel: Custom t-shirts to foster team spirit and safety engagement.

How It Works

We’ve made shopping for promotional items through 911Cellular’s ProShop as easy as possible! If you’re already a valued client, keep an eye on your inbox for an email from our team containing your exclusive link to the store. For new and future clients, you’ll gain access during the onboarding process. Once you receive that link, click it to create your store profile. This will help you effortlessly track any purchases. Now you can start shopping!

All items in our shop are fully customizable, ensuring your promotional materials perfectly match your needs and vision. Check the product listings for all the details about artwork sizing. If you have any questions, want to request that we add new items to the shop or need assistance, our support team is ready to help!

Want to get a head start? You can request access to the shop and dive in ahead of the crowd.

Not quite ready to make a purchase? No worries at all! Our team will give you a friendly reminder during your bi-annual product reviews. And remember, you can always reach out to our support team whenever you’re ready to jump in.

Why a Promotional Storefront?

Imagine a community where everyone is empowered and ready to respond confidently in emergencies! Integrating cutting-edge wearable duress tools, sleek keyboard and wall-mounted panic buttons, innovative mobile safety apps, and robust emergency communication systems is just the beginning. The real magic happens when your community is aware, excited and educated about these life-saving solutions.

To supercharge your institution’s safety culture, we’ve handpicked a dynamic selection of promotional items to spotlight the incredible 911Cellular safety solutions you’ve implemented. This specially designed storefront offers a selection of high-impact promotional items to help you amplify awareness and utilization of the 911Cellular safety products integrated into your security workflow. By increasing awareness, you’ll drive adoption, improve safety culture and ensure your emergency protocols are as effective as possible when it matters most. Let’s inspire everyone to feel prepared and confident when it matters most.

How We Help Our Clients Build a Stronger Safety Culture

At 911Cellular, we understand that fostering a culture of safety requires a comprehensive and consistent approach, along with ongoing training and awareness initiatives—an endeavor that can sometimes feel overwhelming. That’s why we are committed to delivering an exceptional customer support experience before, during and after implementing our safety solutions.

Our promotional items storefront and expert guidance on enhancing the adoption and utilization of our solutions are just a small part of how we support our clients. We also provide personalized onboarding with a dedicated specialist, round-the-clock access to our support team and consistent updates driven by your feedback. Furthermore, biannual check-ins from our system experts and access to educational materials help you get the most out of our systems.

Every day, we collaborate with organizations nationwide to create the safest environments possible, bringing peace of mind to teams so they can focus on what they do best.

Not yet utilizing 911Cellular’s advanced safety solutions? Join us in building a safer future together!

Looking to increase awareness and utilization of 911Cellular’s products across your organization? Request access to the store today and take the next step in enhancing your security culture.
